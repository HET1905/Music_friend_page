Sanam - Indian Pop Music Band

SANAM is an Indian pop rock band formed in 2010 and currently based in Mumbai, India, known for its renditions of old classic Bollywood songs as well as original music. They are also referred to as India's One Direction because of their quick rise in popularity. The band is amongst India's top 10 independent YouTube Channels, India's biggest music artist on the digital platform and the fastest growing YouTube channel in the country.

Sanam has the reputation of being the first Pop Rock Band based in India.

The band was awarded the Best Music Content Creator (National Category) at the Social Media Summit & Awards in Amravati in 2017 by AP Tourism Minister Bhuma Akhila Priya and MP Kesineni Srinivas.
